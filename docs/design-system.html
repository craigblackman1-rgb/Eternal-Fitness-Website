Eternal Fitness — design system

Calm, editorial, studio-led. A wellness brand language built on deep teal, dusty rose, cream, and a serif/sans pairing. Every token and primitive used across the site lives here for review and reuse.

1 · Brand

Logo lockup

EternalFitness

Voice

Calm, confident, never preachy. We coach — we don't sell transformation.

  • · Plain English over fitness jargon
  • · Specific over hype ("steady, sustainable progress")
  • · Respectful of where the reader is right now

2 · Color

A soft pastel palette: peach, blush, rose, sage and mauve. Tokens are authored in oklch; hex values are sRGB approximations for design tooling. Always reference tokens — never hex — in code.
