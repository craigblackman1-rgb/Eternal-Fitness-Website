On Dark

Type on Dark Backgrounds

Dark navy (#282B38) or hero overlay sections use white text with opacity for secondary content.

Heading on Dark — White, Bold Body on dark at 85% opacity for primary content. Muted on dark at 60% opacity for secondary content. Caption on dark at 50% opacity for dates and metadata.
